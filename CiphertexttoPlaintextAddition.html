D-2.5 Ciphertext-to-Plaintext Addition

BFV’s ciphertext-to-plaintext addition uses RLWE’s ciphertext-to-plaintext addition scheme with the sign of the A ⋅S term flipped in the encryption and decryption formulas. Specifically, this is equivalent to the alternative GLWE version’s (§B-4.4) ciphertext-to-plaintext addition scheme (§C-2) with k = 1.
