D-4.4 Ciphertext-to-Plaintext Addition

BGV’s ciphertext-to-plaintext addition scheme is almost the same as BFV’s scheme (Summary D-2.5 in §D-2.5). However, one difference is that it’s not the case that the plaintext polynomial Λ(X) to be added is scaled up by Δ, but it remains as Λ(X).
