D-1.1 Encryption and Decryption

TFHE encrypts and decrypts ciphertexts based on the LWE cryptosystem (§B-2), which is equivalent to the GLWE cryptosystem (§B-4) with n = 1.

𝖫𝖶𝖤s→,σ(Δm) = (a→,b)  ∈ ℤqk+1
