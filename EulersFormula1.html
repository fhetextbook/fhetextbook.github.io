A-11.3 Euler’s Formula

⟨Definition A-11.3⟩ Euler’s Formula

ei𝜃 = cos⁡𝜃 + i ⋅sin⁡𝜃

The value of ei𝜃 is represented as a coordinate on a circle in the complex plane in Figure 3, where the x-axis encodes the value’s real number part and the y-axis encodes the value’s imaginary number part. Note that as 𝜃 increases, the imaginary part (sin⁡𝜃) oscillates between 1 and −1 (reaching i and −i on the imaginary axis), and the real part (cos⁡𝜃) oscillates between 1 and −1, with period 2π.
