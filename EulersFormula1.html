A-11.3 Euler’s Formula

⟨Definition A-11.3⟩ Euler’s Formula

e𝑖𝜃 = cos⁡𝜃 + i ⋅sin⁡𝜃

The value of e𝑖𝜃 is represented as a coordinate on a circle in the complex plane in Figure 3, where the x-axis encodes the value’s real number part and the y-axis encodes the value’s imaginary number part. Note that as 𝜃 increases, the complex part oscillates between i and −i, and the real part oscillates between 1 and −1, with the period of 2π.
