B-6 GGSW

The GGSW cryptosystem is a list of GLev ciphertexts. In the GGSW cryptosystem, the secret key S is a list of k polynomials (i.e., S0,S1,... Sk−1), and each i-th GLev ciphertext in the GGSW ciphertext encrypts the plaintext S0 ⋅M,S1 ⋅M,... Sk−1 ⋅M, and M. This is visually depicted in Figure 10.
