D-3.5.5 Comparing BFV and CKKS Bootstrapping

CKKS bootstrapping shares several common aspects with BFV bootstrapping. CKKSâ€™s ModRaise and Homomorphic Decryption steps are equivalent to BFVâ€™s Homomorphic Decryption (without modulo-q reduction) step. Both BFV and CKKS uses the same strategy for their CoeffToSlot, SlotToCoeff, and Scaling Factor Re-interpretation steps.

Multiplicative Level: A critical difference between BFV and CKKS is that in BFV, the ciphertext modulus q stays the same after ciphertext-ciphertext multiplication, and there is no restriction on the number of ciphertext-ciphertext multiplications.
