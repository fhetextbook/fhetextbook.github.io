D-1.8.8 Discussion
  • Programmable Bootstrapping: While the bootstrapping (Â§D-1.8) uses a simple Lookup Table V (X) which maps Î”m + e to Î”m, we can edit the coefficients of V (X) to make Î”m + e map to different values.   • Bootstrapping Noise: During the bootstrappingâ€™s LUT polynomial V (X) rotation, we perform many TFHE multiplications in the homomorphic MUX gates to derive V 0â‹¯V k, which inevitably creates additional noises before the the noise gets re-initialized at the end.
