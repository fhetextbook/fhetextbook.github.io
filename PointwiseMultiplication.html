A-16.3 Point-wise Multiplication

Once we have applied the forward FFT operation (§A-16.2) to polynomial A(X) and B(X) as y→⟨a⟩ and y→⟨b⟩, computing the point-value representation of C(X) = A(X) ⋅B(X) can be done in O(n) using the Hadamard product y→⟨c⟩ = y→⟨a⟩⊙y→⟨b⟩ (as explained in §A-16.1).
