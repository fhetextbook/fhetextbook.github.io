A-6.2.2 Discussion

Note that after decomposition, the original coefficients of the polynomial have been reduced to smaller numbers. This characteristic is importantly used in the multiplication of polynomials in FHE ciphertexts to reduce the growth rate of the noise. Normally, the polynomial coefficients of ciphertexts are large because they are uniformly random numbers. Reducing such large constants is important for reducing the noise growth during homomorphic multiplication. We will discuss this in more detail in Â§D-1.6.
