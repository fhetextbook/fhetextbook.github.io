A-11.1 Pythagorean Identity

⟨Theorem A-11.1⟩ Pythagorean Identity

sin⁡2𝜃 + cos⁡2𝜃 = 1

Proof.

1.
In Figure 5a, by Pythagorean theorem, a2 + b2 = c2.
2.
In Figure 5b, by definition of sin and cosine,

sin⁡𝜃 = a c, cos⁡𝜃 = b c

3.
Combining step 1 and 2:

sin⁡2𝜃 + cos⁡2𝜃 = a2 c2 + b2 c2 = a2 + b2 c2 = 1

□
