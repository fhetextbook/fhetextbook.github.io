B-1.3 RLWE Cryptosystem

In the RLWE cryptosystem, the formula in ⟨Summary B-1.2⟩ is the same, but s→,a→⟨i⟩,b⟨i⟩,m⟨i⟩,e⟨i⟩ are replaced by polynomials S,A⟨i⟩,B⟨i⟩,M⟨i⟩,E⟨i⟩ as follows:

⟨Summary B-1.3⟩ Lattice-based RLWE Cryptosystem

  • Encryption: B⟨i⟩ = S ⋅A⟨i⟩+ Δ ⋅M⟨i⟩+ E⟨i⟩, where B⟨i⟩ and A⟨i⟩ are publicly known also to the attacker, while S,m⟨i⟩,E are unknown (only known by the secret key owner).

  • Decryption: ⌈B⟨i⟩−S ⋅A⟨i⟩⌋Δ Δ = ⌈ΔM⟨i⟩+ E⟨i⟩⌋Δ Δ = M⟨i⟩ ( provided E⟨i⟩ < Δ 2)

    ⌈⌋Δ is equivalent to rounding each term’s coefficient in the polynomial.
